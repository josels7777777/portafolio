About Me

My name is José Luis Mendoza Aguilar, and I am not just a builder of structures; I am a creator of solutions.

With a foundation built on over 9 years of unwavering dedication to civil engineering, I have embarked on a new journey, one that transcends the conventional boundaries of construction. Fuelled by a deep-rooted passion for merging the realms of civil engineering and computer science, I have set my sights on reshaping the future of construction and real estate.

I grew up in Mexico City, a place filled with social, urban, infrastructural, political, economic, and security issues. This environment taught me the importance as individuals and society to continuously seek for solutions and striving to improve our communities, cities and countries.

As a 28-year-old Civil Engineer, I bridge the gap between the industrial and internet ages. I have a deep understanding of both worlds, a perspective that sets me apart. I've observed a disconnect between generations: the older generation struggles to grasp modern advancements, while the younger generation lacks a complete understanding of historical contexts.

Having recently anchored myself in the vibrant city of Vancouver, British Columbia, I am on a quest to unravel new frontiers, to delve into the depths of computer science, and to emerge as a pioneer in digital innovation. My mission is not just to build structures but to construct a new narrative and change paradigms by solving complex problems and improving situations, driving change in the world.

I acknowledge that as technology progresses, so should our wisdom.
